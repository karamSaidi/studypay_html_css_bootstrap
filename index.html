Efficient School Payments with Innovative Electronic Wallet

An innovative Kuwaiti application, the first of its kind in Kuwait and the Gulf region, serves as an electronic wallet. It seamlessly connects students, parents, and the school administration using advanced technology and a smart application, facilitating their integration into the modern era and artificial intelligence. This transformative solution enables schools to become more digitized by efficiently managing all payments through cutting-edge technology.

Learn More

How it works

With parent’s using the application, students using the smart bracelet, the platform facilitates the financial transaction process. Student Pay makes the payment process easier than ever before, as parents can recharge the balance in seconds via the application, and students can easily pay by scanning the bracelet to the device – or by entering their Student ID Number.
